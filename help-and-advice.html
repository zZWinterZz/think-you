Immediate Crisis Support

If you're in crisis or having thoughts of self-harm, please reach out immediately.

If you or someone you know is experiencing symptoms of any mental health condition, please reach out for professional help.

Crisis Resources:

Emergency Services: 999 (UK)

NHS Non-Emergency: 111 (UK)

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

International: IASP Crisis Centres
