Understanding Mental Health

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act. It influences how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health conditions are real, common, and treatable medical conditions that affect millions of people worldwide. Just as you wouldn't ignore a broken bone or persistent physical pain, mental health symptoms deserve the same attention and professional care.
